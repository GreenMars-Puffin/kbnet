🌿kbnet

Your knowledge base, connected to the GreenMars KB — with your personal notes kept home.

kbnet shares the business knowledge you're already building — meeting notes, projects, clients, ideas — with the GreenMars company KB, so the whole company brain gets smarter together.

Its most important job is keeping your personal notes at home. Mark your personal areas below. Anything that matches stays on this machine, always — it never syncs and is never shared.

GreenMars has no interest in personal information. If something personal ever slips into the company KB, just say so — it will be treated as private, removed, and we'll fix the sorting that let it through.

1 · Your personal folders

Check every folder that holds personal material. Checked folders (and everything inside them) stay home.

2 · Personal tags & markers

Notes tagged with any of these — or with matching frontmatter — stay home too, wherever they live. For one personal paragraph inside a business note, wrap it in a > [!personal] callout and just that section stays home.
